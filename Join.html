Interested in joining?

**You must be a UW-Madison student to join**

Don't miss a thing! Fill out our form to join the email list!

Why Join?

Become a part of our campus-wide event every Fall and a close community year-round!

Details:

Fall Semester:

TEDxUWMadison meets every other Tuesday to watch TED talks and organize our event.

Spring Semester:

TEDxUWMadison meets every other Tuesday to watch TED talks and socialize!
Social events include: bowling, ice cream socials, an event where members can give their own TED talk and more! TEDxUWMadison's opportunities are endless and we would love to add more members to our community.

Member Dues:

Semester: $15

Year: $20

Member dues cover your ticket to the event every fall and most social events.
